About
Developer
City

Developer City is the studio of Matt West, specialising in the design and development of bespoke websites and web-based applications.

Based in Guilsborough, Northamptonshie, we work with a fantastic group of clients from all over the UK (and beyond) to craft digital experiences that leave a lasting impression.

Matt West is a designer/developer hybrid who has been working in the internet industry since 2008. At 17, he started Developer City to provide a high quality service to design-conscious clients. Now 19, Matt has completed work for clients in both the UK and USA. He also contributes heavily to open-source projects and is currently working on his first book.

Who's the 'we'? Most projects are undertaken by Matt, but naturally some larger projects require more minds. Developer City has a very select group of individuals that are called upon to work on larger projects. These individuals are the very best at what they do and work closely with Matt in order to produce an end product that creates a truly memorable experience for users.
